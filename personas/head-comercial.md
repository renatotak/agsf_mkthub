# Persona: Head Comercial & Parcerias

> Sales strategy leader. Uses Market Hub to understand the landscape, identify opportunities, and keep the commercial pipeline healthy.

## Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Decision scope** | Pricing models, partnership terms, sales strategy, territory allocation, commercial KPIs |
| **Data appetite** | Actionable: wants leads, opportunities, and competitive positioning — not raw commodity data |
| **Frequency** | Daily for CRM, 2-3x/week for market intelligence |
| **Tools** | CRM (HubSpot/Salesforce/Pipedrive), LinkedIn Sales Navigator, Power BI |

## Primary Modules

| Module | What they look for | How often |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **CRM & Clients** | Pipeline stages, deal velocity, stalled deals, lead-to-client conversion | Daily |
| **Company Research** | Due diligence on prospects: CNPJ lookup, SWOT, digital presence, news | On-demand |
| **Distribution Channels** | Territory mapping: which states have untapped distributor density? | Weekly |
| **Events** | Upcoming conferences for networking and lead generation | 2x/month |
| **Market Pulse** | Macro context for pricing conversations (commodity trends, FX, Selic) | Weekly |

## Routine: Daily Pipeline Review (9:00 AM)

| Step | Task | Automated / Manual |
|------|------|--------------------|
| 1 | Open CRM module — review deals by stage | **Manual** — CRM data maintained by SDR + Executivo |
| 2 | Check for new leads entered by SDR | **Manual** — SDR enters leads |
| 3 | Review Company Research for any prospects with recent judicial recovery filings | **AI-Assisted** — Recuperacao data auto-synced; cross-referencing is manual |
| 4 | Prepare for calls: pull market context from Market Pulse | **Automated** — data refreshed daily |

## Routine: Weekly Territory Analysis (Wednesday)

| Step | Task | Automated / Manual |
|------|------|--------------------|
| 1 | Open Distribution Channels — filter by state and category | **Manual** — analysis and filtering |
| 2 | Cross-reference with CRM: which high-density states lack active deals? | **Manual** — strategic analysis |
| 3 | Update territory targets for SDR outreach | **Manual** |

## What a Virtual Coworker Can Do

- **Generate prospect research briefs**: Given a CNPJ, compile public data (registration, digital presence, news mentions, SWOT) into a 1-page summary
- **Flag at-risk deals**: Alert when deals have been in the same pipeline stage for 2+ weeks with no interaction logged
- **Territory opportunity reports**: Overlay distribution channel density with current CRM coverage to highlight gaps
- **Event prep packets**: For each upcoming conference, compile attendee companies, relevant news, and talking points
- **Weekly pipeline summary email**: deals progressed, new leads, stalled deals, next actions

## What Requires Human Judgment

- Pricing negotiation and customization
- Partnership terms approval
- Territory allocation strategy
- Deciding which prospects to pursue vs. deprioritize
- Relationship-building and networking at events
