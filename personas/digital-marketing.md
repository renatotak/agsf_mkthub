# Persona: Digital Marketing Analyst

> Content creator and campaign operator. The heaviest daily user of Campaign Center and Content Engine — turns market intelligence into published content and lead-generating campaigns.

## Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Decision scope** | Content calendar, campaign strategy, messaging, SEO/SEM tactics |
| **Data appetite** | Trends and stories: what's moving in agro that AgriSafe can comment on? |
| **Frequency** | Daily — this is their primary workspace for content planning |
| **Tools** | HubSpot, Semrush/Ahrefs, Google Ads, LinkedIn Campaign Manager, Meta Business Suite, GA4, Canva |

## Primary Modules

| Module | What they look for | How often |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Campaign Center** | Campaign pipeline: what's in draft, planned, active, completed? | Daily |
| **Content Engine** | AI-generated content ideas scored by market relevance | Daily |
| **Agro News** | Today's headlines — source material for social posts, blog articles, newsletters | Daily |
| **Market Pulse** | Commodity trends to frame in blog posts (e.g., "Soy up 3% this week — what it means for credit risk") | 3x/week |
| **Competitor Radar** | What are competitors publishing? Gaps to exploit? | Weekly |

## Routine: Daily Content Workflow (9:00 AM)

| Step | Task | Automated / Manual |
|------|------|--------------------|
| 1 | Scan Agro News for today's top stories | **Automated** — news synced daily, categorized automatically |
| 2 | Check Content Engine for AI-generated idea suggestions | **AI-Assisted** — ideas generated, human selects and refines |
| 3 | Pick 1-2 stories to turn into social media posts | **Manual** — creative writing and brand voice |
| 4 | Review Campaign Center: any campaigns moving to active today? | **Manual** — campaign management |
| 5 | Draft blog post outline based on Market Pulse data | **AI-Assisted** — market data automated; outline drafting can be AI-assisted |

## Routine: Weekly Content Planning (Monday)

| Step | Task | Automated / Manual |
|------|------|--------------------|
| 1 | Review past week's content performance (external analytics) | **Manual** — GA4, LinkedIn, etc. |
| 2 | Pull this week's Content Engine ideas ranked by trend score | **AI-Assisted** — trend scores auto-calculated |
| 3 | Plan editorial calendar in Campaign Center | **Manual** — strategic planning |
| 4 | Scan Competitor Radar for competitor content gaps | **Manual** — competitive analysis |
| 5 | Assign content pieces to production pipeline | **Manual** |

## What a Virtual Coworker Can Do

- **Generate daily social media drafts** based on top Agro News headlines, aligned with AgriSafe's 3 pillars
- **Write blog post first drafts** using Market Pulse data as the hook + Content Engine idea as the angle
- **Create weekly content performance summaries** from external analytics APIs (if connected)
- **Suggest optimal posting times** based on historical engagement data
- **Auto-tag content ideas** with SEO keywords derived from current commodity trends
- **Draft newsletter sections** combining this week's market moves, news highlights, and upcoming events

## What Requires Human Judgment

- Final brand voice and tone approval for all published content
- Creative direction for visual assets (Canva, video)
- Campaign budget allocation and ad spend decisions
- Choosing which stories align with AgriSafe's current strategic messaging
- Relationship management with external agencies and media
