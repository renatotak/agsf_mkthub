# Persona: CEO / Managing Partner

> Strategic visionary. Needs the 10,000-ft view of market conditions, competitive landscape, and pipeline health to make portfolio and partnership decisions.

## Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name archetype** | Renato (Managing Partner) |
| **Decision scope** | Portfolio composition, strategic partnerships, executive approvals, investment priorities |
| **Data appetite** | High-level KPIs, trends, and anomalies — not raw data |
| **Frequency** | Weekly check-ins, on-demand when preparing for board/investor meetings |
| **Tools** | Google Workspace, LinkedIn, Power BI |

## Primary Modules

| Module | What they look for | How often |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Dashboard** | Stats overview: commodities tracked, campaigns active, CRM pipeline size, active modules | Weekly |
| **Market Pulse** | Macro trends: USD/BRL, Selic, commodity price direction — is the market favoring AgriSafe's positioning? | Weekly |
| **Competitor Radar** | Any big moves? New funding, product launches, partnerships by TerraMagna, Traive, Agrotools, etc. | Weekly |
| **CRM** | Pipeline funnel health: how many leads in each stage? Any stalls? | Bi-weekly |

## Routine: Weekly Strategic Review (Monday AM)

| Step | Task | Automated / Manual |
|------|------|--------------------|
| 1 | Check Dashboard for high-level stats | **Manual** — open and scan |
| 2 | Review Market Pulse for commodity price trends and macro indicators | **Automated** — data refreshed daily by cron |
| 3 | Scan Competitor Radar for new signals | **AI-Assisted** — competitor signals auto-ingested; interpretation is manual |
| 4 | Glance at CRM pipeline stage distribution | **Manual** — CRM data entry is team-driven |
| 5 | Skim Agro News highlights (if time) | **Automated** — curated by cron + category classification |

## What a Virtual Coworker Can Do

- **Generate a weekly executive briefing** summarizing: top 3 commodity moves, any competitor alerts, pipeline changes, and recommended actions
- **Flag anomalies** automatically: sudden commodity price drops >5%, new competitor funding announcements, pipeline stages with zero movement for 2+ weeks
- **Prepare board deck data slides** by pulling latest Market Pulse + Competitor Radar data into a structured summary

## What Requires Human Judgment

- Portfolio prioritization decisions
- Partnership approval/rejection
- Investment allocation between product lines
- Interpreting competitor moves in the context of AgriSafe's strategy
