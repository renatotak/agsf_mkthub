# Persona: Consultor Senior de Estrategia e Mercado

> Market strategy expert. Uses Market Hub to gather intelligence for client deliverables — market sizing, competitive landscaping, supply chain analysis.

## Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Decision scope** | Analysis recommendations, methodology choices, project framing |
| **Data appetite** | Deep analytical: needs time series, comparisons, and source traceability |
| **Frequency** | 2-3x/week, intensively during project phases |
| **Tools** | Excel/Sheets, PowerPoint/Slides, Power BI/Tableau, CONAB/IBGE/MAPA/BACEN databases |

## Primary Modules

| Module | What they look for | How often |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **Market Pulse** | Commodity price history, macro trends for client presentations | Per-project |
| **Competitor Radar** | Competitive landscape for sector analysis | Per-project |
| **Company Research** | Public due diligence on companies in a client's value chain | On-demand |
| **Agro News** | Sector trends and recent developments to cite in reports | 2-3x/week |
| **Distribution Channels** | Channel structure analysis per state for GTM recommendations | Per-project |

## Routine: Project Kick-off Research (Ad hoc)

| Step | Task | Automated / Manual |
|------|------|--------------------|
| 1 | Pull commodity price trends for the relevant crops | **Automated** — data available in Market Pulse |
| 2 | Map competitive landscape using Competitor Radar | **AI-Assisted** — signals auto-collected; synthesis is manual |
| 3 | Research key companies in the value chain via Company Research | **Manual** — CNPJ lookups require judgment on which entities matter |
| 4 | Pull distribution channel data for target states | **Automated** — retailers data pre-loaded |
| 5 | Compile into client deliverable (deck/report) | **Manual** — requires consultant expertise |

## What a Virtual Coworker Can Do

- **Generate market landscape briefs**: Given a commodity or sector, compile price trends, key players, recent news, and regulatory developments into a structured brief
- **Prepare competitive benchmarking tables**: Pull all competitors from Radar, format as comparison matrix with latest signals
- **Extract distribution channel statistics**: Count distributors by state, category, and classification for a given region
- **Draft value chain diagrams** using distribution channels + competitor data
- **Monitor specific keywords** for a project: set up temporary keyword tracking and alert when relevant news appears

## What Requires Human Judgment

- Strategic diagnosis and recommendation formulation
- Client-specific contextualization (their competitive position, internal capabilities)
- Methodology selection and analytical framing
- Interpreting qualitative market signals (industry sentiment, regulatory direction)
- Final deliverable quality and narrative
