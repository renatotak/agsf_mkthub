# Persona: SDR Analyst (Sales Development Representative)

> Lead generation and qualification specialist. Uses Market Hub to identify prospects, research companies before outreach, and qualify leads using market context.

## Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Decision scope** | Lead qualification scoring, outreach messaging customization |
| **Data appetite** | Prospect-focused: company info, sector trends, buying signals |
| **Frequency** | Daily — core workflow depends on Market Hub data |
| **Tools** | LinkedIn Sales Navigator, CRM (HubSpot/Pipedrive), Apollo.io, Hunter.io, Google Alerts |

## Primary Modules

| Module | What they look for | How often |
|--------|-------------------|-----------|
| **CRM & Clients** | Current pipeline, lead stages, last interactions — avoid duplicate outreach | Daily |
| **Company Research** | CNPJ lookup before outreach: what does this company do? Who are the key people? | Daily |
| **Distribution Channels** | Identify potential prospects by state, category, and classification tier | Daily |
| **Agro News** | Conversation starters: "I saw your sector had [news event], here's how AgriSafe helps" | Daily |
| **Market Pulse** | Market context for BANT qualification (Budget: "With Selic at X%, credit conditions are Y...") | 2-3x/week |

## Routine: Daily Prospecting Workflow (8:30 AM)

| Step | Task | Automated / Manual |
|------|------|--------------------|
| 1 | Check CRM: any leads requiring follow-up today? | **Manual** — CRM review |
| 2 | Scan Distribution Channels for new prospect targets in assigned territory | **Manual** — filtering and selection |
| 3 | Research each prospect via Company Research (CNPJ, digital presence) | **AI-Assisted** — public data available; interpretation is manual |
| 4 | Check Agro News for relevant stories to personalize outreach | **Automated** — news curated daily |
| 5 | Draft personalized outreach messages | **Manual** — personal touch essential for B2B sales |
| 6 | Log new leads in CRM with qualification score | **Manual** — data entry |

## Routine: Weekly Prospect List Building (Monday)

| Step | Task | Automated / Manual |
|------|------|--------------------|
| 1 | Filter Distribution Channels: target state + category + classification A/B | **Manual** — strategic targeting |
| 2 | Cross-reference with CRM to exclude existing contacts | **Manual** |
| 3 | Build prospect list of 20-30 new companies | **Manual** |
| 4 | Enrich with Company Research data | **AI-Assisted** — public data compiled automatically |

## What a Virtual Coworker Can Do

- **Generate prospect research cards**: For each CNPJ, compile: company size, sector, state, digital presence, recent news mentions, whether they appear in judicial recovery filings
- **Auto-match news to prospects**: "3 articles this week mention companies in your target territory"
- **Draft outreach email templates** personalized with market data hooks ("Soy prices dropped 4% this week — here's how our credit risk scoring helps distributors in SP")
- **Build lead enrichment reports**: Combine Distribution Channels data with Company Research to create prioritized prospect lists
- **Flag high-priority leads**: Companies recently mentioned in news (expansion, new partnerships) that match AgriSafe's ICP

## What Requires Human Judgment

- Final qualification scoring (BANT: Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline)
- Outreach tone and personalization (relationship-building)
- Deciding which prospects to prioritize based on strategic fit
- Phone/video call conversations
- Escalation decisions (when to hand off to Executivo de Contas)
